# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Skills Board is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community. Be constructive, assume good intent while staying accountable for impact, and focus criticism on ideas and work rather than people.

Examples of positive participation include:

- giving specific, actionable feedback;
- respecting different experience levels, backgrounds, and tool choices;
- acknowledging mistakes and repairing harm;
- keeping discussions relevant to the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, doxxing, or publishing another person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate this standard and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and centered on community safety.

Report conduct concerns privately through [GitHub private reporting](https://github.com/TommyBez/skillsboard/security/advisories/new). Include “Code of Conduct” in the report title. Do not publish sensitive conduct reports in an issue.

This code applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing the project.
